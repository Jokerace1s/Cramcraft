Paste your flashcard data below. Each complete flashcard (term/question + definition/answer) should be a block of text.

The term/question can span multiple lines (e.g., for multiple-choice options).

The definition/answer should be on the last line of its block, separated from the term/question part of that line by the last instance of your chosen separator.

A new flashcard block starts on a line that is NOT a multiple-choice option (e.g., not starting with "a.", "b.", etc.).

Example (using Comma):

What is the capital of France?,Paris

Example (using Tilde):

Common fruits~Apples, Oranges, Bananas

Example (using Custom Separator, e.g., '|'):

Term with pipe|Definition with pipe
